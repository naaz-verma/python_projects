# WorldWithWeb - Sessions & Programs

All sessions operate on **3 Levels** across **2 audience segments**.

| Level | Purpose | Format |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Level 1** | Inspiration & Awareness | Free talks (1-1.5 hrs) |
| **Level 2** | Skill Building | Paid bootcamps / recurring sessions |
| **Level 3** | Career & Personal Development | Add-on program (6 sessions) |

| Segment | Audience | Focus |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **School** | Classes 8-10 | Python, AI, cybersecurity, game dev -- build from fundamentals |
| **College & Professional** | College students, early professionals | Market-ready skills -- automation, web, marketing, AI tools |

# SCHOOL SEGMENT (Classes 8-10)

---

## Level 1: Inspiration Talk -- "What Can You Build?"

**Duration:** 1-1.5 hours | **Goal:** Excite students, demo live projects, funnel into 6-week program

### Structure:

1. **Opening** -- "What did you use AI for today?" (interactive)
2. **Live demos** -- Password cracker, network scanner, AI chatbot (5 projects)
3. **The 3 levels** -- User vs Builder vs Creator
4. **Why study still matters** -- AI amplifies, doesn't replace
5. **Portfolio pitch** -- "Start before college, be ahead of everyone"
6. **Interactive close** -- "What would YOU build?"

*(Full demo session plan and PPT available in the project repo)*

---

## Level 2: The 6-Week Program + Biweekly Special Sessions

### Core Program: 6-Week Python Track

Students attend regular classes (2-3x/week) following a phased curriculum:

| Phase | Weeks | Focus | AI Role |
|-------|-------|-------|---------|
| Foundation | 1-2 | Python fundamentals, logic, problem-solving | None |
| Building | 3-4 | APIs, web apps, OOP, AI integration | Learning tool |

*(Detailed curriculum in README.md)*

### Biweekly Special Sessions (Recurring, Every Batch)

These run alongside the 6-week program. Self-contained -- students can miss one without falling behind. Repeat for every new batch.

#### Category 1: Tech Awareness (Discussion + Demo, No Coding)

| # | Session | Hook | What They Learn |
|---|---------|------|-----------------|
| S1 | **How the Internet Actually Works** | "What happens in 0.3 seconds between typing google.com and seeing the page?" | DNS, HTTP, servers, IP, packets -- demystify what they use daily |
| S2 | **AI: What It Actually Is (and Isn't)** | "ChatGPT doesn't think. Let me show you what it actually does." | Tokens, probabilities, training data, hallucinations -- cut the hype |
| S3 | **How Apps Make Money** | "Instagram is free. So how is it worth $100 billion?" | SaaS, freemium, ads, data models -- think like a founder not a consumer |
| S4 | **Tech Careers Nobody Tells You About** | "50+ job roles in tech. You've heard of 3." | DevOps, product, UX, security, data engineering -- expand their mental map |
| S5 | **Open Source & How Software Is Really Built** | "The code behind your browser, your OS, even AI -- it's all public." | GitHub, open source culture, how real teams ship software |

#### Category 2: Hands-On Skill Sessions (Workshop, Build Something)

| # | Session | Hook | What They Build |
|---|---------|------|-----------------|
| S6 | **Linux & The Terminal** | "Every hacker, server, and Android phone runs Linux." | Basic commands, file navigation, WSL setup, terminal confidence |
| S7 | **Git & GitHub: Your Code Portfolio** | "How every developer in the world saves and shares code." | GitHub account, push first project, understand commits and repos |
| S8 | **HTML + CSS: Build Your Website** | "Build your personal website. Today. For free." | Single-page portfolio site, deployed on GitHub Pages |
| S9 | **Web Scraping with Python** | "Pull any data from any website automatically." | Scrape live data (weather, cricket scores, prices) with Python |
| S10 | **Build a Telegram/Discord Bot** | "A bot that runs in your actual group chat." | Working bot that responds to commands -- friends can use it instantly |
| S11 | **Prompt Engineering Workshop** | "Same AI, wildly different results. The difference is how you ask." | Hands-on prompt crafting, comparing outputs, building a prompt library |
| S12 | **APIs: Connecting Everything** | "Weather apps, payment gateways, AI -- it's all APIs." | Call 3 public APIs, display results, understand request/response |

#### Category 3: Challenge Sessions (Competitive, High Energy)

| # | Session | Hook | Format |
|---|---------|------|--------|
| S13 | **CTF Day (Capture The Flag)** | "Real cybersecurity challenges. Crack codes, find hidden flags." | Beginner CTF from picoCTF or TryHackMe, teams compete |
| S14 | **Debug Battle** | "I wrote broken code on purpose. First team to fix all bugs wins." | Timed debugging challenges, progressively harder, tournament bracket |
| S15 | **AI vs Human Quiz-Off** | "Can you write a better answer than ChatGPT?" | Students and AI both answer questions, class votes -- critical thinking |
| S16 | **Mini Hackathon** | "2 hours. Build something. Anything. Best project wins." | Teams of 2-3, build a small project, present to peers, vote on winner |

# COLLEGE & PROFESSIONAL SEGMENT

---

## Level 1: Inspiration Talks -- "Digital + AI: Skills for the Real World"

**Duration:** 1-1.5 hours | **Audience:** Entire college / mixed batches | **Goal:** Mindset shift + funnel into Level 2

### Talk Option A: Digital Marketing & AI -- Building Your Online Presence

- Visibility > silence -- why every student needs a digital presence
- Digital marketing + AI for every student (tech and non-tech)
- Mini hands-on: Build a real online presence in the session
- Flagship talk -- works for any college, any department

### Talk Option B: Tech + Portfolio = Future-Proof Career

- Why skills alone aren't enough anymore
- Portfolio > marks (with real examples)
- GitHub, LinkedIn, personal websites -- what recruiters actually look for
- AI tools that make you 10x productive

### Talk Option C: AI Demystified -- From Hype to Real Skills

- What AI actually is (and isn't) -- cut through the noise
- Live demo: Build an AI app in 15 minutes
- Jobs AI creates vs jobs AI replaces
- How to position yourself on the right side

**Outcome:** Students feel included, colleges see relevance, natural upsell to Level 2 bootcamps.

## Level 3: Career & Personal Development Program

**Name:** "Career Readiness & Confidence Program"

**Format:** 6 sessions (can run weekday evenings or weekend slots)

**Audience:** Works for both school (classes 9-10) and college students. Adjust examples and depth per audience.

| Session | Topic | What They Practice |
|---------|-------|--------------------|
| 1 | **English Confidence** | Speaking exercises, thinking in English, removing hesitation |
| 2 | **Communication & Body Language** | Presentations, eye contact, professional tone, group discussions |
| 3 | **Resume & Email Writing** | Write a real resume, draft professional emails, cover letters |
| 4 | **Personality & Mindset** | Growth mindset, handling failure, discipline vs motivation |
| 5 | **LinkedIn & Digital Presence** | Build/optimize LinkedIn, personal branding, network building |
| 6 | **Mock Interviews + Feedback** | Live mock interviews, peer feedback, confidence building |

**Outcome:** Confidence, employability, professional communication. High value for tier-2/3 college students and school students preparing early.
